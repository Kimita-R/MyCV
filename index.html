About Me

I’m a fullstack developer based in South Africa.

I like to believe that there is a solution to every problem and I strive to bring this attitude to every project I work on.

Having worked for the LEGO Certified Stores SA as a developer and business systems analyst and currently as a Web Designer in the eBucks FNB team – I am passionate about provided customer centric tech solutions to meet a range of business requirements. A solution is only as good as the experience of the people who use it. Working in the fast pace of retail, ecommerce and tech I relish the challenges that come with an ever-changing and competitive environment. I enjoy the continued journey of learning, be it new technologies, methods of problem solving or how the needs of business and customers continue to evolve.

My goal is to use analytical and logical skills paired with the soft skills of understanding the user experience to create solutions that are as efficient, effective, and user-friendly as possible.
